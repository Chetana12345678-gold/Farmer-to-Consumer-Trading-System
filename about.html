ABOUT FARMER TO CONSUMER

Farmer To Consumer

Farmer to Consumer is an online platform that connects farmers directly with customers. It removes the middlemen and helps farmers to get fair prices for their products while providing fresh goods to consumers.

The system provides features like product listing, order management, and user-friendly navigation. It ensures transparency, better pricing, and efficient communication between farmers and consumers.
